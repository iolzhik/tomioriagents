
# Meta Ads Implementation Guide (Luxury & Jewelry)

## 1. Campaign Setup & Objectives
- **Objective:** Sales (Conversions) or Leads. For luxury jewelry, 'Sales' with a 'Purchase' conversion event is preferred.
- **Conversion Location:** Website or WhatsApp.
- **Advantage+ Campaign:** Use only for retargeting or broad testing. Manual sales campaigns offer better control for luxury targeting.

## 2. Audience Targeting (Astana & Luxury Segment)
- **Location:** г.Астана (ТРЦ "Керуен" радиус 10км) + High-income neighborhoods.
- **Detailed Targeting:**
    - Interests: Luxury goods, Diamond, Engagement ring, Rolex, Private banking.
    - Life Events: Recently engaged (1 year, 6 months, 3 months), Upcoming anniversary (within 60-90 days), Friends of recently engaged people.
    - Behavior: Frequent International Travelers, Owners of iPhone 15/16 Pro.
- **Exclusions:** Drop-shippers, "Job: Wedding Photographer" (unless targeting B2B).

## 3. Creative & Visuals (MCP Standards)
- **Format:** Carousel (for collections), Single Image (for high-impact hero items), Reel (for showroom tours).
- **Hooks:**
    - Macro-shots of diamond sparkle.
    - "Unboxing" experience.
    - "Посетите нас в ТРЦ \"Керуен\", 2 этаж".
- **Overlay:** Minimalist brand logo in the corner. No heavy text on images.

## 4. Ad Copy (AIDA Model)
- **Attention:** "Exclusive diamonds in the heart of Astana."
- **Interest:** "GIA certified gems and bespoke craftsmanship at Tomiori."
- **Desire:** "Experience the brilliance of our new 'Astana Night' collection."
- **Action:** "Посетите наш шоурум в ТРЦ \"Керуен\" или напишите нам для персональной консультации."

## 5. Bidding & Budgeting
- **Budget:** Start with $20-50/day per ad set.
- **Bidding:** Highest volume for cold audiences. Cost cap for warm/retargeting.
- **Pixel:** Must be active with 'ViewContent', 'AddToCart', and 'Purchase' events.
